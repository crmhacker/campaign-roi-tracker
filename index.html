Revenue by Model

Touches per Opp

ROI Formula: (Attributed Revenue − Campaign Spend) ÷ Campaign Spend → ROI%.
Spend: Ad cost + media fees (+ optional SDR/AE cost). Revenue: closed-won amount × attribution share.
Models: First-Touch (FT), Last-Touch (LT), Linear (even split), W-Shaped (FT, Lead-Create, Opp-Create weighted; e.g., 30/30/30 and 10% to assists).
